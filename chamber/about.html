About Us

Welcome to the Chamber of Commerce of Blantyre, Malawi. We are dedicated to fostering business growth, innovation, and collaboration across various industries in our community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support businesses in Blantyre by providing networking opportunities, advocating for favorable policies, and offering resources to help local enterprises thrive in a global market.

Our History

Founded in 2024, the Chamber of Commerce of Blantyre has been a vital force in the business community. Over the years, we have supported hundreds of local businesses through training programs, industry-specific events, and valuable connections.
