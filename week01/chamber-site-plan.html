Site Name

Blantyre Chamber of Commerce

This name reflects the local area and emphasizes the chamber's role in promoting business and community engagement in Blantyre, Malawi.

Site Purpose

The Blantyre Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Facilitate networking opportunities among local businesses.
  • Provide resources and information to support economic growth and tourism.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The selected color palette includes:

  • Dark Green: #006400 (used for headings and backgrounds)
  • Gold: #FFD700 (used for accents and buttons)

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Poppins: Used for headings.
  • Lato: Used for body text.

Wireframe

A rough sketch of the homepage layout will include:

  • Header: Logo and navigation links.
  • Main Content Area: Hero section, membership levels, events section.
  • Footer: Contact information and social media links.
