Welcome to Lilongwe Chamber of Commerce

Project Overview

You are about to embark on the creation of the Lilongwe Chamber of Commerce website. This site plan will guide the development process.

Location Selection

We have chosen Lilongwe City, the heart of Malawi, as the location for our Chamber of Commerce project. This vibrant city is our home.

Setting Up Your Project

To start, we've created a new HTML file named "chamber-site-plan.html" within the "week01" directory. We've also added a CSS file for styling.

Content Structure

Site Name:

The site will be known as "Lilongwe Chamber of Commerce," a name that reflects our city and purpose. We've chosen this name to represent our commitment to Lilongwe's business community.

Site Purpose:

The Lilongwe Chamber of Commerce website has the following purposes:

    Education and Training Hub

    Purpose: Offer online courses, workshops, and resources to enhance skills and knowledge, empowering individuals for career growth.

    Export Promotion

    Purpose: Support local businesses in exporting their products, boosting the national economy.

    Healthcare Access

    Purpose: Provide information about healthcare services, clinics, and public health initiatives, improving overall well-being.

    Community Engagement

    Purpose: Facilitate community involvement in local development projects, encouraging citizen participation and volunteerism.

    Government Relations

    Purpose: Act as a liaison between local businesses and government entities, advocating for favorable policies and regulations.

Scenarios:

Our website will answer key questions for our visitors, including:

    Investment Opportunities

    Scenario: "What are the current investment opportunities in Malawi?"

    Purpose: Attract foreign and local investors to support economic growth.

    Tourist Information

    Scenario: "Can I find details about tourist attractions and accommodations?"

    Purpose: Promote tourism by providing information on Malawi's natural wonders and hospitality.

    Job Listings

    Scenario: "Are there job listings and career resources available?"

    Purpose: Assist job seekers and connect local talent with opportunities.

    Business Networking

    Scenario: "How can I network with local businesses for collaboration?"

    Purpose: Facilitate connections among businesses for mutual growth and partnerships.

Color Schema:

We've carefully selected our color scheme:

    Nature-inspired

    Colors: Earthy greens, warm browns, and sky blues.

    Purpose: Reflect the natural beauty and resources of Malawi, promoting eco-friendly initiatives and tourism.

    Cultural Vibrancy

    Colors: Rich reds, deep purples, and golden yellows.

    Purpose: Embrace the cultural diversity of Malawi, celebrating traditions, festivals, and artistic expressions.

    Innovation Hub

    Colors: Modern, clean, and tech-inspired colors like blues, grays, and electric greens.

    Purpose: Highlight Malawi's potential for technological advancement, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

Typography:

We've chosen fonts that capture Lilongwe's spirit:

  • Headings: "Nunito", Arial, sans-serif - Modern and uniquely Lilongwe.
  • Body Text: "Nunito", Arial, sans-serif - Comfortable and legible for all.
